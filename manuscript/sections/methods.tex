\subsection{Data sources}
% ============================================================================

Proteomics data, genome annotations, and the uTP HMM profile were obtained from Coale et al. \citep{coale2024}. The \textit{B. bigelowii} transcriptome contained approximately 44,000 predicted proteins.

Haptophyte proteomes were downloaded from NCBI GenBank: \textit{Emiliania huxleyi} CCMP1516 (GCA\_000372725.1, $\sim$38,500 proteins), \textit{Chrysochromulina tobinii} CCMP291 (GCA\_001275005.1, $\sim$16,700 proteins), \textit{Diacronema lutheri} (GCA\_019448385.1, $\sim$14,400 proteins), \textit{Pavlovales} sp. CCMP2436 (GCA\_026770615.1, $\sim$26,100 proteins), \textit{Prymnesium parvum} 12B1 (GCA\_041296205.1, $\sim$23,700 proteins), and \textit{Prymnesium} sp. SGEUK-05 (GCA\_046255225.1, $\sim$15,000 proteins).
